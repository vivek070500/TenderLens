CENTRAL RESERVE POLICE FORCE
OFFICE OF THE DIG, GROUP CENTRE, CRPF, NEW DELHI

TENDER NOTICE

Tender No: CRPF/GC/NDL/CONST/2026-27/001
Date of Issue: 01 April 2026
Last Date for Submission: 30 April 2026

Subject: Construction of Administrative Building and Residential Quarters at CRPF Campus, Gurugram, Haryana


SECTION 1: SCOPE OF WORK

1.1 The Central Reserve Police Force invites sealed tenders from eligible contractors for the construction of a two-storey administrative building (approx. 12,000 sq ft) and 24 residential quarters (Type-III) at CRPF Campus, Sector 47, Gurugram, Haryana.

1.2 The estimated cost of the project is Rs. 8,50,00,000/- (Rupees Eight Crore Fifty Lakhs only).

1.3 The project must be completed within 18 months from the date of award of contract.


SECTION 2: GENERAL INSTRUCTIONS

2.1 The tender documents can be downloaded from the CRPF website and the Central Public Procurement Portal (CPPP).

2.2 Bids must be submitted in two parts: Technical Bid (Part I) and Financial Bid (Part II). Only those bidders who qualify in the Technical Bid will have their Financial Bid opened.

2.3 The tender evaluation committee reserves the right to reject any or all bids without assigning any reason thereof.

2.4 All disputes shall be subject to the jurisdiction of courts in New Delhi.


SECTION 3: EARNEST MONEY DEPOSIT (EMD)

3.1 The bidder must submit an Earnest Money Deposit (EMD) of Rs. 10,00,000/- (Rupees Ten Lakhs only) in the form of a Demand Draft or Bank Guarantee from any scheduled commercial bank in India, drawn in favour of "Commandant, Group Centre, CRPF, New Delhi".

3.2 The EMD of unsuccessful bidders will be returned within 30 days of finalisation of the tender. The EMD of the successful bidder will be adjusted against the security deposit.

3.3 Bids submitted without EMD shall be summarily rejected.


SECTION 4: FINANCIAL ELIGIBILITY

4.1 The bidder must have an average annual turnover of not less than Rs. 5,00,00,000/- (Rupees Five Crore only) during the last three financial years (FY 2023-24, FY 2024-25, and FY 2025-26), as certified by a Chartered Accountant. Audited balance sheets and profit and loss statements for all three years must be submitted.

4.2 The bidder must have a positive net worth as on 31st March 2026. A certificate from a Chartered Accountant confirming the net worth shall be enclosed.

4.3 The bidder should not have been blacklisted by any Central Government or State Government department, Public Sector Undertaking, or autonomous body at any time during the last five years.


SECTION 5: TECHNICAL ELIGIBILITY AND EXPERIENCE

5.1 The bidder should have successfully completed at least three (3) similar works, each of value not less than Rs. 2,00,00,000/- (Rupees Two Crore only), during the last five (5) years ending on 31st March 2026. "Similar works" shall mean construction of buildings (residential or commercial) for Central Government, State Government, or Public Sector organisations.

5.2 Work completion certificates from the client organisation must be submitted for each claimed project. Self-certified project lists without supporting completion certificates will not be accepted as standalone evidence.

5.3 The bidder must have adequate technical personnel on their rolls, including at least one qualified civil engineer with minimum 10 years of experience.

5.4 The bidder must own or have access to the construction equipment and machinery necessary for the execution of the work.


SECTION 6: COMPLIANCE REQUIREMENTS

6.1 A valid registration under the Goods and Services Tax (GST) Act is mandatory. A copy of the GST Registration Certificate showing active status must be submitted.

6.2 A valid Permanent Account Number (PAN) issued by the Income Tax Department is mandatory. A copy of the PAN card must be submitted.

6.3 ISO 9001:2015 certification from an accredited certifying body is required. The certificate must be valid as on the date of submission of the bid.

6.4 The bidder must have valid registration with the Central Public Works Department (CPWD) or the Military Engineer Services (MES) or any State PWD in an appropriate class. A copy of the registration certificate must be submitted.


SECTION 7: ADDITIONAL EVALUATION CRITERIA (DESIRABLE)

7.1 Experience in construction projects for paramilitary or defence establishments is desirable and will carry additional weightage of up to 10 marks in the technical evaluation.

7.2 Possession of ISO 14001:2015 (Environmental Management) certification is desirable and will carry additional weightage of up to 5 marks.

7.3 Track record of completing projects ahead of schedule will be viewed favourably.


SECTION 8: DOCUMENTS TO BE SUBMITTED

The following documents must be submitted as part of the Technical Bid:

  a) Company registration certificate / Certificate of incorporation
  b) Audited balance sheets and P&L statements for FY 2023-24, 2024-25, and 2025-26
  c) CA certificate of average annual turnover
  d) CA certificate of net worth
  e) Work completion certificates for similar works claimed
  f) GST Registration Certificate
  g) PAN Card
  h) ISO 9001:2015 certificate
  i) CPWD / MES / State PWD registration certificate
  j) EMD in the form of DD or Bank Guarantee
  k) Affidavit of non-blacklisting on a stamp paper of Rs. 100/-
  l) Details of technical personnel with qualification certificates
  m) List of equipment and machinery owned or available


SECTION 9: EVALUATION PROCESS

9.1 The Technical Bid will be evaluated based on the eligibility criteria specified in Sections 3 through 6. Bidders who meet all mandatory criteria will be declared technically qualified.

9.2 Technically qualified bidders will be further scored on the desirable criteria in Section 7.

9.3 Financial Bids of only those bidders who score a minimum of 60 marks out of 100 in the technical evaluation will be opened.

9.4 The contract will be awarded to the lowest qualified bidder (L1) subject to the bid being within the estimated cost.
